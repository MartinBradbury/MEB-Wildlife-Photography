Welcome

Hi, I'm Martin!

An Amature Photographer

I am currently learning and developing my skills as a landscape and wilflife photographer.

I have always had an interest in nature and the ecosystems that sustains them. Whilst out hiking you are able to capture nature in its purest form.

I bought my first DSLR camera in 2022 and now enjoy taking photographs of nature and I want to share what I capture with you.

Being Lincoln based I am traveling to all parts of the country where I have the opportunity to developing my photography skills whilst capturing moments of natural tranquility, peace and calmness.
