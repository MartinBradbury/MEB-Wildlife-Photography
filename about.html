Welcome to my page

My name is Martin and I am currently learning and developing my skills as a wildlife and scenic photographer. I have always had an interest in wildlife, especially when hiking in the Peak District and Lake District. I bought a camera in 2022 and now enjoy weekends taking photographs of the world around me and I want to share what I capture with you. Being Lincoln based I have a short trip over to the Peaks where I am harnessing my photography skills and proud of the natural photographs that capture moments of peace, calmness and joy.
